Who We Are

Building Connections & Transforming Lives in Ghana

Be My Friend Foundation is a faith-based NGO rooted in Afienya, Ghana. We were founded on the belief that every child — regardless of background — deserves the love of the gospel, quality education, and a caring community to help them flourish.

We connect caring volunteers with children in underprivileged communities, nurturing their spiritual growth, inspiring their minds, and supporting their development into compassionate, knowledgeable, and confident individuals.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Two guiding lights that shape every programme, every visit, and every friendship we build.

🎯

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the love and teachings of the gospel while providing educational opportunities and encouragement to young children. We aim to nurture their spiritual growth, inspire their minds, and support their development into compassionate, knowledgeable, and confident individuals. Through faith-based programs and educational initiatives, we strive to create a positive and lasting impact on the lives of children and their communities.

🔭

Our Vision

Our vision is to see a world where every young child is empowered through faith and education, growing in their understanding of the gospel and developing the skills needed to thrive. We envision a future where children are encouraged to explore their potential, embrace their values, and become leaders in their communities, spreading hope and positivity wherever they go.

Our Commitment

Aims & Objectives

Clear goals that hold us accountable and drive every decision we make for the children we serve.

1

To promote the teachings of the gospel among young children in a nurturing environment.

2

To provide quality educational resources and support that foster holistic development.

3

To encourage positive values and character building in children through faith-based initiatives.

1

To organize regular gospel-centered programs and activities that engage children and teach them about faith.

2

To develop and implement educational workshops and resources that enhance learning in key subjects such as literacy, numeracy, and life skills.

3

To create mentorship programs that connect children with positive role models who can guide and inspire them.

4

To collaborate with families and communities to support the educational and spiritual growth of children.

5

To evaluate and improve our programs continuously to ensure they meet the evolving needs of the children we serve.

Our Programmes

What We Do

Every initiative is designed to plant seeds of faith, knowledge, and hope in young hearts.

🙏

Gospel Outreach

Regular gospel-centered programs and activities that engage children and teach them about faith in a joyful, nurturing environment.

📚

Educational Support

Workshops and resources enhancing learning in literacy, numeracy, and life skills — giving every child the tools to thrive academically.

🤝

Mentorship Programs

Connecting children with positive role models who guide, inspire, and walk alongside them as they grow in confidence and character.

🌱

Community Collaboration

Partnering with families and communities to create a holistic support system for the educational and spiritual growth of children.

💡

Character Building

Faith-based initiatives that nurture positive values — compassion, integrity, resilience — shaping children into tomorrow's leaders.

🎉

Community Events

Celebratory gatherings like our Amazing Shepherd event that create joyful memories and strengthen the bonds of community.
